Farkon Kwanaki Bayan Haihuwa

Bayan haihuwa, jariri yana buƙatar kulawa ta musamman. A cikin farkon kwanaki, yana da mahimmanci a kula da yanayin zafi, yanayin bacci, da yadda ake ciyar da shi. Jariri na iya kasancewa cikin rashin kwanciyar hankali a wannan lokacin, saboda haka ya kamata a ba shi kulawa mai kyau.

Shawara Mai Sauƙi

A farkon kwanaki, jariri yana bacci tsawon sa'o'i 16-17 a rana. Yi amfani da wannan lokacin don hutawa da kanku.

Wasu abubuwan da ya kamata a lura da su a farkon kwanaki sun haɗa da:

  • Yanayin zafi na jiki
  • Yanayin bacci
  • Yadda ake shaƙa
  • Yanayin fitsari da kashi

Yadda Ake Ciyar Da Jariri

Nonon uwa shine mafi kyawun abinci ga jariri a farkon watanni shida na rayuwarsa. Yana ba da duk abubuwan gina jiki da jariri yake buƙata, gami da antibodies masu kare shi daga cututtuka.

Lokutan ciyarwa na iya zama daga sau 8-12 a rana a farkon kwanaki. Yana da mahimmanci a ba jariri damar sha nono da yawa, musamman a farkon kwanaki bayan haihuwa lokacin da nonon ke samar da colostrum mai yawan sinadirai.

Sanin Alamun Yunwa

Alamun yunwa na jariri sun haɗa da: motsin baki kamar yana nono, jujjuyawar kai daga gefe zuwa gefe, da ɗan kukan mara ƙarfi.

Kula da Cikakken Bacci

Jariri yana bacci tsawon sa'o'i 16-17 a rana a farkon watanni. Duk da haka, baccin yana zuwa ne a cikin lokuta gajeru, yawanci tsawon sa'o'i 2-4.

Don taimaka wa jaririnki ya sami cikakken bacci:

  1. Yi masa lullaby ko rera waƙa mai sanyin jiki
  2. Yi amfani da sutura mai dacewa don yanayin zafi
  3. Kiyaye yanayi mai natsuwa da duhu lokacin bacci
  4. Yi masa shafa a hankali ko lallaba

Tsaftar Jariri

Kula da tsaftar jariri yana da mahimmanci don hana cututtuka. A cikin farkon kwanaki, ba a ba da shawarar yin wanka jariri kowace rana ba, amma a yi amfani da sponge don tsaftace shi.

Lokacin canjin bantar jariri, yi amfani da tawul mai laushi da ruwan dumi don tsaftace shi, sannan a shafa man shafawa don hana rashin lafiyar fata. A kowane lokacin da kika canji bantar jariri, ka tabbata an tsaftace shi daga gaba zuwa baya musamman ga 'ya'ya mata.

Alamomin Lafiya da Rashin Lafiya

Yana da mahimmanci a san alamomin lafiya da rashin lafiya na jariri. Wasu alamomin lafiya sun haɗa da:

  • Sha nono da kyau
  • Yin fitsari da kashi akai-akai
  • Nuna alamun nishaɗantarwa

Alamomin rashin lafiya da ya kamata a kalli sun haɗa da:

  • Rashin sha
  • Zazzabi (sama da 38°C)
  • Rashin fitsari ko kashi
  • Kukan marar dacewa

Shawarwari na Musamman

Ga wasu shawarwari na musamman don kula da jariri bayan haihuwa:

Kula da Yanayin Zafi

Jariri ba ya da ikon kula da yanayin zafin jikinsa sosai. Tabbatar cewa yana sanye da tufafi masu dacewa da yanayin.

Tsarin Bacci Mai Amfani

Kafa tsarin bacci na yau da kullun don taimaka wa jariri ya sami isasshen bacci.

Hana Cututtuka

Yi wa jariri allurar rigakafi bisa ga jadawalin da likita ya tsara don kare shi daga cututtuka masu saurin yaduwa.

Kula da jariri bayan haihuwa na iya zama abin ƙalubale, amma kuma yana da ɗabi'u masu yawa. Ka tuna cewa kowane jariri na da bambanci, don haka kada ka damu idan jaririnka bai yi kamar ɗan uwansa ba ko kuma jaririn abokinka.

Bar Sharhinka

Fatima Yusuf

Maris 18, 2024

Na gode da wannan labari mai matukar amfani. Ni dai na samu wahalar kula da jaririna a farkon kwanaki, amma bayan karanta wannan labarin na fahimci yadda zan inganta kula da shi.

Amina Musa

Maris 16, 2024

Labari mai matukar dacewa ga duk uwa mai sabon jariri. Ina son yadda aka bayyana dukkan matakan kula da jariri a bayyane. Zan ba da shawarar ga 'yan uwana masu juna biyu.
